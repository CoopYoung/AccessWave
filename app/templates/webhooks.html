Webhooks

Get notified when scans complete. AccessWave will POST a signed JSON payload to your endpoint whenever a scan finishes or fails.

Example payload & signature verification 
{
  "event": "scan.completed",
  "scan_id": 42,
  "site_id": 7,
  "site_name": "My Website",
  "site_url": "https://example.com",
  "status": "completed",
  "score": 87.5,
  "pages_scanned": 5,
  "total_issues": 12,
  "critical_count": 1,
  "serious_count": 3,
  "moderate_count": 5,
  "minor_count": 3,
  "started_at": "2026-03-29T10:00:00",
  "completed_at": "2026-03-29T10:00:42"
}

Verify the X-AccessWave-Signature header:
sha256=HMAC-SHA256(secret, raw_request_body)
